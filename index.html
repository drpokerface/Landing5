Introducing Opinion Wars


People on the internet love to argue. What if there were financial stakes involved? Similar to how people make money by betting on the right companies in the stock market,what if we could something similar for opinions? The goal of opinion wars is:


  1. to create a decentralized platform where the market can decide on how strong the overall sentiment towards a given opinion in order to give an honest indication of the public sentiment regarding various important topics

  2. to create a similar system that rewards those who bet on the right ideas, while penalizing those who bet on wrong ones similar to how people make/lose money by betting on the right/wrong companies in the stock market

  3. to create an innovative new kind token associated with the protocol that addresses some of major issues faced by other cryptocurrencies designed to be as lucrative as possible for holders



How it works


Any user will be able add any tweet to opinion wars board and stake any amount of BNB either in favor or against the tweet. For example say there exists a tweet saying “Bitcoin will remain to be the most popular cryptocurrency over the next 10 years”. Now anyone who agrees with the tweet can add this tweet to opinion wars and stake any amount of BNB in favor of this tweet. After the tweet is added others also will be able stake either in favor or against the tweet. All the tweets on opinion wars will be ranked in a descending order of total stake. Once the timer hits zero, the side that has more stake wins. If majority of the stake is for the tweet then the overall sentiment is considered positive, and everybody who voted in favor of tweet wins. And if the opposite happens everyone who voted against the tweet wins. And the the BNB of the losers get distributed to the winners in proportion to their winner bet sizes.
